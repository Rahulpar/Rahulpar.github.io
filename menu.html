MENU

Mom had a spice box in her kitchen, all the tasty dishes that used to come out of her kitchen had one or more ingredients from her spice box. When I got curious about cooking, I started using her spice box.

Over the years, my friends and colleagues tasted many of these dishes created from my cook book, they encouraged me to share those dishes to more food lovers.

I am carrying all of Mom's recipies in a hand written book and made a new spice box with all the same spices Mom used to use.

Below is our Menu, few authentic dishes carved out of the Good Old Cook Book.
